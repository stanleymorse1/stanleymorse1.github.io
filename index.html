Introduction

I'm Stanley Morse, a graduate game design student who's always in search of a new challenge. I'm usually a programmer, but I like to dip my toes into other disciplines like sound design, music, and 2D/3D art from time to time.

About Me

I'm a twenty-something indie game developer based in London, England with a passion to create games ranging from chill puzzle platformers to fast-paced narrative adventures. From a young age I've been frantically drawing and doodling ideas almost daily and dreaming of the games I would make. Since I began my first game project for my A-level Computer Science coursework in 2017, I've been making those dreams a reality.
