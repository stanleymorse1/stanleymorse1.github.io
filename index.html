About Me

My Face

I'm a twenty-something indie game developer based in London, England with a passion to create games ranging from chill puzzle platformers to action-packed narrative adventures. From a young age I've been frantically drawing, jotting ideas and dreaming almost daily of the games I would make. Since I began my first game project for my A-level Computer Science coursework in 2017, I've been making those dreams a reality.

I've always worked without following tutorials where I could, instead abstracting and breaking down each problem into its component parts and using documentation to assist in finding a solution. This helped me to develop my problem-solving skills and enables me to solve and implement unique problems and experimental features in my games. Paired with my perseverance, I am yet to find a problem I couldn't solve (at least, within game development).
