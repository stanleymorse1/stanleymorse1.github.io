About

Hello

I'm a twenty-something indie game developer based in London, England with a passion to create games ranging from chill puzzle platformers to action-packed narrative adventures. From a young age I've been frantically drawing, jotting ideas and dreaming of the games I would make. Since beginning my first game project for Computer Science coursework in 2017, I've been making those dreams a reality.

I'm self-taught and rarely use tutorials, instead abstracting and breaking down problems into simpler components to find a solution. This helped me to develop my problem-solving skills and enables me to tackle unique problems and implement experimental features in my games. Paired with my perseverance, I am yet to find a problem I couldn't solve (at least, within game development).
