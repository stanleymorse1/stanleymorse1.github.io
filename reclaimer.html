Reclaimer

Solo project – all assets are my own aside from some character animations

Reclaimer is a work-in-progress action rogue-lite that follows a guardian golem in restoring order to a dystopian, magic-fearing civilisation.

The game features twin-stick controls, where the player can move and attack in different directions, block, dash, cycle through and execute abilities, designed with controller support in mind. There are many ability combos that can be used, such as slashing while blocking to break the shield in an AOE attack that expels its stored damage. It features other complex systems such as character IK for procedural hit reactions, dithering of vision-obscuring walls and objects, and modular AI statemachines.

Reclaimer’s AI enemies are smart and optimized; they use line-of-sight detection to track and investigate the player’s last known position without cheating, utilizing realistic search behaviours to allow for tactical stealth gameplay. The AI are optimized to reduce their simulation rate beyond a certain distance from the player, so far away or off-screen enemies won’t use as much CPU time but will still be able to track and respond to allies.

Reclaimer is in early development
