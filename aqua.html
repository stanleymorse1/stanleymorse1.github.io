Aqua

Tech lead, graphics and gameplay programmer, some level and mechanical design

A short 2D puzzle adventure game where the player must use the power of a water spirit and their platforming skills to solve puzzles and save the world. I programmed our two character controllers, including multiple complex animation condition and state changes.

The water spirit character uses a procedurally animated sprite culling mask for the transition animations to make the thawing animation correctly orient to the relevant heat source.

Aqua was a team effort, I worked with multiple other talented individuals to make the game in just under 4 months. We later spent a further month polishing it for submission to the Aesthetica Film Festival, for which it is currently nominated for an award.
